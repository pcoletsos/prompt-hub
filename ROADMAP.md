## Long-term Vision

### Goals

1. **Comprehensive Prompt Library**
   - Cover all major use cases
   - High-quality, tested prompts
   - Clear documentation and examples
   - Attribution and versioning

2. **Structured Learning Hub**
   - Complete learning paths for AI/LLM topics
   - Curated resources at all levels
   - Practical, hands-on content
   - Up-to-date with latest research

3. **Active Community**
   - Regular contributions
   - Knowledge sharing
   - Peer review and feedback
   - Collaborative improvement

4. **Trusted Resource**
   - Known for quality and accuracy
   - Cited by educators and practitioners
   - Referenced in courses and books
   - Industry recognition

### Success Metrics

- [ ] 100+ high-quality prompts
- [ ] 50+ learning resources
- [ ] 20+ active contributors
- [ ] 1000+ GitHub stars
- [ ] Featured in AI newsletters/blogs
- [ ] Referenced in courses
